###### I am a Juan de la Cierva Postdoctoral Fellow at the Theory division of the [Institut de Física d'Altes Energies (IFAE)](https://www.ifae.es/) in Barcelona. My work is on strong gravity, black hole physics and gravitational waves. I am specially interested on the interplay between strong gravity and beyond Standard Model physics, including the dynamics of light bosons around compact objects. This has a direct application to dark matter: gravity continues to be the most promising (and perhaps the only) way to shed light into the longstanding problem of the nature of dark matter.
