I'm a Juan de la Cierva Postdoctoral Fellow at the Theory division of the [Institut de Física d'Altes Energies (IFAE)](https://www.ifae.es/) in Barcelona. My work focus on strong gravity, black hole physics and gravitational waves. I'm specially interested in the interplay between gravity and beyond Standard Model physics, in particular, on how can we use black holes and other compact objects to learn more about dark matter. Gravity is the most promising (and perhaps the only) way to shed light into the long-standing problem of the nature of dark matter.
